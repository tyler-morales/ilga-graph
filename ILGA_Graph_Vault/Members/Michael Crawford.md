## Biography
Postsecondary educator and administrator. Born and raised in Chicago, graduate of Lane Technical High School. B.S./M.S.Ed., Northern Illinois University; MBA, Saint Xavier University; Ph.D., Concordia University Chicago. Lives in Chicago with his wife and two children.
